# collectiv

Collectiv – The Smart Pigmy Collection App

Collectiv is a modern Flutter-based mobile application designed to digitize and streamline the traditional daily pigmy collection process. Built for field agents, micro-finance institutions, and small business owners, Collectiv replaces manual paper ledgers with a secure, real-time digital platform.

Why Collectiv?
Traditional pigmy collection is prone to errors, lost records, and time-consuming calculations. Collectiv empowers agents to manage thousands of transactions effortlessly, ensuring 100% transparency between the collector and the customer.

Key Features:

Effortless Daily Collection: Mark daily payments with a single tap. Track paid, pending, and overdue accounts instantly.

Customer Management: Maintain a comprehensive digital directory of all your pigmy customers, complete with contact details and historical records.

Route Optimization: Plan your daily collection route efficiently to save time and fuel costs.

Real-time Analytics: Get instant insights into your daily collections, total outstanding amounts, and commission calculations without manual math.

Digital Receipts: Generate immediate SMS or digital receipts for customers, building trust and accountability.

Cross-Platform Sync: Powered by Flutter, Collectiv offers a smooth, native-like experience on both Android and iOS devices.

The Collectiv Advantage

Offline Ready: Work seamlessly in areas with poor internet connectivity; data syncs automatically when you are back online.

Secure & Reliable: Protect your financial data with robust encryption and local backup features.

User-Friendly Interface: Designed for agents of all tech levels, ensuring a minimal learning curve.
